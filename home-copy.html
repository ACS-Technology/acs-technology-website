FAQ

Your questions answered simply

Get clear answers about our web design and development services for small businesses.

How much does a website cost?

Our packages start at $599 for a Launch site, $999 for a Growth package, and $1,499 for our Pro package with chatbot integration. Clear, upfront pricing — no surprises.

What happens after I place my order?

Once payment is complete, you’ll receive a short onboarding form to collect your business details, text, and photos. As soon as we have your info, we’ll start building your site — most sites are ready to launch in about 1 week.

How long will it take to build my site?

Most sites are completed in about a week, depending on the package you choose and how quickly you provide your content.

Will my site work on mobile phones?

Yes — every site we build is fully responsive and works beautifully on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Can I use my own domain name?

Absolutely. We can connect your existing domain or help you register a new one.

Do you provide hosting?

We’ll make sure your site goes live smoothly. We can transfer your site to your own Webflow account (so you handle hosting directly), or we can host it for you.

Do you offer updates after my site launches?

Yes — we include a 30-day free revision period after launch to cover quick text edits, image changes, or small design tweaks. After that, you can either request updates as needed or choose an ongoing plan if you prefer regular support.
